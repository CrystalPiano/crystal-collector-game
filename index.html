Crystal Collector

Here's how you play the game


  1. Below is a random number, generated at the start of each game.
  2. Also below, are four crystals.
    • Red
    • Blue
    • Yellow
    • Green
  3. By clicking a crystal, you will add the associated value to your total score.
  4. You win if you are able to match your score to the random number.
  5. You lose if your score goes over the random number.
  6. The value of each crystal is a secret until you click on it, but changes each new game.
